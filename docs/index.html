Limpid Chess

A calm chess game for beginners. Every turn shows three moves, your job is to find the best one.

Get it on Google Play Android · launching soon · free, with an optional one-time Premium

Made for people who freeze each turn

Instead of a blank board of thirty legal moves, you get three clear choices and learn to spot the good one.

Three moves, every turn

The best move, a "not bad" move, and a blunder, drawn as plain numbered arrows. You pick; then the colours and a coin reveal how you did.

Calm and kind

Soft colours, no clocks, no shame on losing. Mistakes are the lesson, never the punishment.

Bots beginners can beat

Opponents tuned for real first-timers and kids, getting tougher as you grow, with a relative difficulty meter, not a scary rating.

Offline, no ads, no accounts

One small save on your device. No tracking, no sign-in, ever. Read the position, find the move, that's it.

How it works

No blank board of thirty options, just three arrows. Pick one, and the answer reveals itself.
